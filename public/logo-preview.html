Three directions · Emerald & Gold palette

Option A The Seal

Circular medallion with 8-pointed star (Rub el Hizb) frame and the letter Qaf — the first letter of Qur'aan.

Option B The Arch

A Mihrab arch — the prayer niche in mosques — with ق inside. Instantly recognisable as Islamic and scholarly.

Option C The Open Book

An open book — the Qur'aan itself — with Arabic script lines on one page and ق on the other. Most literal, warm and inviting.
